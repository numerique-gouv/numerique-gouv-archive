L’État dans le nuage : la journée dédiée au cloud dans la sphère publique

Le 18 mars prochain, l’État vous invite à plonger au cœur des enjeux du cloud dans la sphère publique. La troisième édition de l’événement “L’État dans le nuage” promet une journée riche en échanges, retours d’expérience et ateliers pratiques.

Partager

À l’occasion de cet événement réunissant de nombreux intervenants de référence, la DINUM invite l’ensemble des professionnels du monde de la tech et de la sphère publique à partager leurs expertises sur l’évolution des enjeux stratégiques du Cloud en France comme en Europe.

An event dedicated to cloud computing in the French public sector

On Tuesday, March 18th, the third edition of L’État dans le nuage will take place - an event dedicated to cloud computing in the public sector. The Interministerial Digital Directorate (DINUM), in charge of the French Government’s cloud strategy, is pleased to invite you to engage with key stakeholders and speakers on the latest cloud-related topics, at the crossroads of technology and public administration.

Download the agenda

Morning sessions

Keynote speakers will share their views regarding the latest strategic developments in France and Europe:

  • An overview of the French Government’s Cloud Strategy
  • A European roundtable on other national strategies and the issue of autonomy and strategic sovereignty in Europe
  • A perspective on the challenges of cloud technology in the defense sector
  • A case study on cloud transformation in the private sector

Afternoon sessions

Following a buffet lunch, attendees will be able to pick and choose sessions from 3 tracks:

  • Strategic & managerial topics, including recent regulatory developments in France and Europe, security, sustainability, governance.
  • Case studies & post-mortems from various publics entities. They will be sharing their cloud transformation journey and learnings, covering a wide range of topics: move to cloud strategy, landing zones, containerization.
  • Technical workshops and demos, such as an introduction to infra-as-code and kubernetes.

This event being first and foremost dedicated to the cloud community, you will be provided with ample opportunities for informal discussions throughout the day, as well as dedicated corners presenting different offerings to address 1:1 any question you may have.
